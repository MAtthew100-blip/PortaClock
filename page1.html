The PortaClock is unlike any other, you can carry it anywhere. 

Each Clock has three removeable components, designed for the most pristine portability, better than any full-sized clock on the planet.

1

Fluoropolymer Plastic cover

Our prestigious, detachable fluoropolymer plastic cover secures your PortaClock's internal pieces, and is designed with a suction seal which is installed on our Deluxe and Waterproof designs, as well as outstanding weather resistance and shock tested material tested from 10 feet. 

2

Removeable Hands

PortaClock comes with three attachable hands made from EVA plastic to provide powerful flexibility combined with a durable frame. Available in black and blue, black and red and black and green colour schemes. 

3

Replaceable, Chargeable Battery 

Each PortaClock has a 3000 mAh battery manually installed to ensure a use duration of at least 6 months until the battery needs to be recharged or replaced. Each battery is water resistant up to 10 meters as well as shock resistant up to 8 meters. The battery is enclosed in a graphene-hybrid material fused into a cobweb netowork, then layered with thermoplastic sheets to ensure battery security and safety. A 3500 mAh battery is used in the deluxe design.
